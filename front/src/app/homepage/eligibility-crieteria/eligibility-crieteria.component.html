Eligibility Criteria

To donate blood, individuals must meet specific criteria as outlined below: Ensuring eligibility involves meeting requirements such as age, weight, vital signs, absence of respiratory diseases, adequate hemoglobin levels, healthy skin condition, and a clean medical history. These criteria ensure the safety and efficacy of blood donations for both donors and recipients alike.
